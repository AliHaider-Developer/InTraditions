Kund Malir Beach

Introduction

Kund Malir Beach is a stunning coastal destination located along the Makran Coastal Highway in Balochistan, Pakistan. Known for its crystal-clear waters, golden sands, and peaceful surroundings, it is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the country.

Scenic and Natural Beauty

  • Pristine Shoreline: The beach offers a long stretch of clean, untouched sand bordered by turquoise waters.
  • Mountain Backdrop: Surrounded by rocky hills and desert terrain, it provides a dramatic and serene landscape.
  • Sunsets: The sunsets at Kund Malir are a major attraction, often creating breathtaking views for photography and relaxation.

Accessibility and Location

Located about 236 km from Karachi, Kund Malir lies within Hingol National Park. The drive along the Makran Coastal Highway is scenic and passes through rugged cliffs and coastal plains, making the journey as memorable as the destination.

Nearby Attractions

  • Princess of Hope rock formation
  • Hinglaj Mata temple
  • Balochistan Sphinx
  • Hingol River and National Park

Travel Tips

There are limited facilities near the beach, so visitors are advised to bring food, water, and camping gear if planning an extended stay. The best time to visit is from October to March for cooler, pleasant weather.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Makran Coastal Highway, Balochistan
  • Distance from Karachi: Approx. 236 km
  • Ideal Time to Visit: October to March
  • Activities: Sightseeing, photography, picnicking, and camping
  • Nearby Sites: Princess of Hope, Hingol National Park
